About Azzurra

Our Journey

Driven by Science.
Guided by Purpose.

Azzurra Pharmaconutrition Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2014 with a singular conviction: that clinical nutrition, when formulated with true scientific rigour, can transform the trajectory of human health. We are not a wellness brand — we are a pharmaconutrition company, built by scientists for clinicians and their patients.

Our brand philosophy is rooted in the divine inspiration of Sky Blue — a colour that represents protection, life, and the infinite possibilities of science. Nothing is more precious than Human Lives. Every formulation we create carries this responsibility.

Operating from Faridabad (NCR-Delhi), India, we work in technical collaboration with Azzurra Pharmaconutrition, USA — bridging global research with the local clinical needs of patients across India.

⚕️ Image: Company / team photo

2014

Azzurra Pharmaconutrition Pvt. Ltd. founded in Faridabad, India with a vision to advance clinical nutrition through science.

2016

Technical collaboration established with Azzurra Pharmaconutrition, USA — importing global research expertise into Indian clinical practice.

2018

Launch of the Essential and Vital Series — foundational product lines addressing elemental nutritional needs in clinical settings.

2020

Expansion into critical care nutrition with the Max Series, developed in response to clinical demands during the global health challenge.

2023

AAECEN association strengthened; team of 18 clinical nutrition specialists driving ongoing R&D and product innovation.

2025

Six complete product series, 50+ clinical formulations, and a growing commitment to reaching healthcare providers globally.

Our Foundation

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in clinical nutrition globally.

We envision a world where every patient — regardless of geography or clinical complexity — has access to nutrition solutions that are scientifically validated, precisely formulated, and genuinely effective.

Our Mission

Reaching Globally, Understanding Locally.

Innovation and research to bring happiness to life. We combine world-class science with a deep understanding of local clinical realities — formulating solutions that work where they are needed most.

Our Culture

Nurturing Minds

We believe our people are our greatest formulation. A culture of curiosity, care, and commitment drives everything we do. Our team of scientists, nutritionists, and clinical experts are united by a single purpose — advancing human health through evidence-based nutrition.

At Azzurra, we invest in knowledge, foster collaboration across disciplines, and hold ourselves to the highest standards of integrity — in our science, in our products, and in our relationships with the clinicians and patients who trust us.
